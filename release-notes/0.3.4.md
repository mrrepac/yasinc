First public release of **yasinc** — two-way synchronization of an Obsidian
vault with **Yandex Disk** over its official REST API. Works on desktop and
mobile (everything goes through Obsidian's `requestUrl`, no third-party
servers).

Highlights:

- **Two-way sync** with sha256-based diff and a last-sync snapshot, so
  "deleted" is told apart from "created on the other side".
- **Manual and automatic**: ribbon button, hotkey, sync on startup, on a timer,
  and auto-push of edits after a quiet period.
- **Safety first**: conflicts keep both copies (`.conflict-…`), deletions go to
  the trash, downloads are checksum-verified, and several brakes guard against
  mass deletion (a per-device snapshot id and a bulk-delete confirmation).
- Traffic-light status bar, a sync log, a size cap, and a master on/off switch.

The token is stored in app local storage, never inside the vault.
